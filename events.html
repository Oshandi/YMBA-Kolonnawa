Events & Activities

Organized and conducted Volleyball training camp promoting sport activities for schools in Kolonnawa division. (March 2010)

YMBA (K) organized Blood Donation Camp at HQ. (February 2010)

Collected and distributed Dry -rations, clothes, books and other urgent household items valued over Rs. 300,000/- to the flood victims in the Kalutara,Galle and Ratnapura Districts. (May 2003)

YMBA (K) held its "Website launching" ceremony at the Sri Vimalaramaya Temple at Wellampitiya in the presence of a large and distinguished gathering. A seminar was also conducted to the school children by the Sri Lanka TeleCom. Officials on the subject of Web-Sites. (May 2003)

Share certificates were officially handed over to the shareholders of YMBA (K)'s Siri Suwasetha Medical Center. (March 2003)

Held the "foundation laying ceremony" for the Head Quarters Building. (January 2003)

Organized and held a conference of Chief Priests of all temples in the Kolonnawa electorate at the Kolonnawa Purana Raja Maha Viharaya.
Ven Dr. Baddegama Samitha (Member of Parliament) also attended this conference as an observer. Guest Speaker - Ubayawansa Warnakulasooriya of Ernst & Young Colombo. (January 2003)

Participated in the conducting and supervising of the YMBA Annual Examination at 25 examination centres in the Kolonnawa electorate. (October 2002)

Received from the government the "title deed" of the land where the new Head Quarters Building is coming up at Kolonnawa-Vihara Mawatha Junction. (July 2002)

Conducted Wesak Day -Buddhist Devotional Songs (Bhakthi Geetha) Inter School Competition. A program of awarding life membership at "once-and-for-all" membership fee of Rs.1,000/- was also launched during the Wesak Season. Rs. 80,000/- has been collectected under this program. (May 2002)

Conducted Free Medical Clinic-treated about 300 patients. (January 2002)
