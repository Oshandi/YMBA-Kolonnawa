Services

YMBA (K) assists in numerous ways to promote education, art, literature, sports and other life skills of children and the youth of the area through seminars and workshops, class room teaching, public lectures on a calendar of continuing monthly programs and annual events.

YMBA (K) has been of assistance to the YMBA Colombo in providing supervisory and coordinating functions connected with the annual Dhamma examination in the centers of Kolonnawa electorate. This is an island-wide examination of Buddhist Spiritual Education.

Suwasetha Medical Centre

YMBA (K)'s Medical Center situated at the headquarters building of YMBA (K) is a profitable venture that offers OPD services, specialist channel services, pathological laboratory services and pharmacy services at concessionary rates. Dental & Eye care Units were attached to the medical center recently, expand the serveices provide by Suwasetha medical center.

The Medical Center also provides free services to the needy and disabled children and specially the helpless senior citizens.

This project set up in August 2001 is a success and has great prospects of development and expansion. Its share capital is heal on 50:50 basis by the YMBA (K) and the individual members of the association.

Sambuddhaloka Pre-School

YMBA (K) operated pre-school situated at Orugodawatta where community comprised of low income families in need of a pre-school for their children who comes from all religions.

Presently about 100 children have been enrolled for the academic year 2010/2011 and these children our next generation taught and taken care by well trained qualified teaching staff which compose of three communities sinhala, tamil & muslim.

YMBA (K) with a vision enlightening the next generation as a future investment for the well-being of society, generously spent around Rs. 30,000.00 to operate and maintain this facility. So your generous support and donations are welcome too.

Head Quarters Building Project

Having realized the potential for expansion of its activities in scope, volume and intensity taking advantage of the recent turn-around of the country's socio-economic outlook and peace among the communities,the YMBA (K) wishes to have a permanent Head Quarters building for which it has been able to secure a block of land on long term lease from the government in a prime location in Kolonnawa.

The first stage construction of the new building has completed and the YMBA (K) medical center operate at a section of the building and the payments recive as monthly rental has support to build a costant growing fund for the YMBA (K).
Other in-house educational and sports activities and monthly religious programs also organize within the premises too.
